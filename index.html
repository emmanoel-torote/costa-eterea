Where Desert Meets Ocean

Located in the sought-after Las Tunas neighborhood of Todos Santos, Costa Eterea blends contemporary design with the natural beauty of the desert and ocean. This is not just a home—it's an experience.

Designed by renowned architect Diana Zavala and built by Baja Integrity Builders, every detail has been thoughtfully crafted to create a sanctuary where form meets function.

The ground floor hosts three beautifully appointed bedrooms, offering comfort and privacy. Ascend to the second level where an open-concept kitchen flows seamlessly into the living area and outdoor kitchen—perfect for entertaining under the Baja sky.

But the crown jewel awaits on the third floor: a rooftop lounge with a cushioned swing, where breathtaking sunsets over the Pacific and Sierra mountains become part of your daily ritual.
